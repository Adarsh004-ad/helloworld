About Adarsh Precast

Located in the heart of Siwan, Bihar, Adarsh Precast has established itself as a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality precast concrete products. Founded by Mr. Manan Singh, our company operates on the principles of quality, durability, and customer satisfaction.

We specialize in a diverse range of products, including concrete door frames, ventilators, paver blocks, boundary walls, and various drainage solutions. By leveraging modern manufacturing techniques in a controlled factory environment, we ensure that every product meets stringent quality standards, offering superior strength and a flawless finish.

Our mission is to support the growth of our region by providing reliable, cost-effective, and innovative construction materials. Whether you are a large-scale builder or an individual homeowner, Adarsh Precast is your trusted partner for building a stronger tomorrow.
